Identity, Values and Strengths Exploration

Prior to really going deep and thinking about what Identity, values and strengths meant to me, I decided to take the Myers Briggs test. Although the results were not surprising at all, I ended up doing more research on what each attribute meant and found out I’m a perfectionist (which can be a good and bad personality trait to have). My personality type is Mediator INFP – T. In other words I scored high in being an Introvert, being Intuitive, following my feelings when making decisions and emotions, being prospecting when approaching work, planning and decision-making, and lastly having a turbulent personality.
