Understanding Neuroplasticity and how it benefits other people and my own learning.

Understanding that neuroplasticity allows connections between neurons in our brain, benefit people in ways that if we were to repeatedly practice an activity or access a memory, the neurons would group and fire together, creating electrochemical pathways that shape themselves according to that activity or memory.
Ways to use the principles of neuroplasticity for my own learning experience is to practice and go over tasks as said above. This will help build and strengthen the connections between the neurons in my brain. Actively recalling the memory of something will make it easier to recall the memory each time when thought of.

Here are some ways to increase ones Neuroplasticity:

  • Getting enough sleep. Your brain need sleep in order to reset connections that are important for memory and learning.
  • Continuing learning and keep moving
  • Reducing stress in your life
  • Finding a strong purpose for what you’re planning to learn
  • Taking time to read a novel or book

What and why is Growth mindset relevant

Growth mind-set is recognising your abilities can be developed and engaging them deeply. These students process the error, learn from it and correct it. Without a growth mind-set students have a fixed mind-set, which means they run from their errors instead of embracing an engaging with them. They will take other paths like cheating or finding someone with a lower ability than you and compare themselves to make themselves feel better. This is relevant because if you are wanting to develop growth, you need to have a growth mind-set, so instead of praising intelligence or talent , we should praise the process that people engage in.

Did anything surprise me?

Through this exploration I think having Carol Dweck explain that praising one’s intelligence and talent can make you fail in the long run really didn’t surprise me. I think personally praising ones intelligence can deteriorate another’s confidence and cause them to find other solutions as said above.

How will I integrate growth mindset into my learning journey?

I think having a positive mind-set and being able to identify your mistakes will help you learn from them and adapt. Learning from experience is the best way to start growth mind-set, because without understanding where your errors are you cannot grow.
