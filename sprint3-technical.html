When trying to understand what the differences of HTML and css are, try thinking of it as building a house. You have the frame, walls, roof, bedrooms, entrance, kitchen, etc., this would be your HTML. Whereas the styling, such as decorations, how tall walls are and the colour of them, would be the CSS.
